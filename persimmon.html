Freehand guide

I call this a "freehand guide" because the most magical part of knitting is fitting your knits exactly to your body and style! Take the following steps as a guide :) My measurements are 33"-27"-35" and I casted on stitches based on wanting 2 inches of negative ease at my waist. That just means casting on 27" - 2" = 25" for the 2x2 rib.

Time for stockinette! Increase by a certain % depending on your preferred chest fit.

For one cutot:

(feel free to change the number of increases/decreases depending on your preferences, just keep them even!)

Bind off 2 in the front. 2 total decreases, 3 total increases. Decrease with 3 stitches left in the row, and increase with 2 stitches left on the row. At each beginning, slip the first stitch. Decreases: K2Tog, Increases:Join: K2Tog with slip stitch behind. Do the increase/decrease on every other row.



ğŸŒ¸   Step 4: Top ribbing

2 options:

1) Twisted rib: Top 2x2 ribbing with twiste: 2x2 rib with knit st through the back loop.

2) Folded hem: Knit normal ribbing for double the final length you want. Once you reach your desired length, foldover and bind off.



ğŸŒ¸   Step 5: Bind off

Regular bind off with the following modifications (inspired by Lori's Twisty Bind Off): Before every knit stitch, twist needle clockwise. Before every purl stitch, twist needle counter clockwise. After one purl, yarn over counterclockwise and pass YO + previous stitch over new stitch
